The Martial Artist

This martial arts company sells high quality and a very diverse sets of equipment for almost every martial art needs. They are also known to be great at custom embroidery for robes and any other equipment able to be personalized. This logo and brand is a redesigned version to give the brand a more modern and timeless look. The brand package along with the logo consists of a complete branding guide booklet, a letterhead, a business card, and an envelope.
